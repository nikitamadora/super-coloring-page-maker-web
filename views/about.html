What is the Super Coloring Page Maker?

Tl;dr: An interactive random coloring page generator!

Intention

Super Coloring Page Maker! is designed to generate random assortments of shapes and patterns, so that anyone can have a canvas to color when the mood strikes! It's also a personal project that started simply, and has plenty of room for growth, so I can tinker on it over time!

Inspiration

The idea came to me during the Covid-19 shelter-in-place. While I have plenty of coloring books to color me happy, I know others do not, and that's a tragedy. I wanted to create a simple coloring page generator so that anyone with access to a printer or a tablet would have a place to start their coloring journey!
